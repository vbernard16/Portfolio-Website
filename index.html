Projects

AI-TUNES

Welcome to AI-Tunes. Select your favorite artist and try your luck at guessing which song the AI generated voice is singing. A team collaborated, full stack application utilizing the MERN stack and various third party API's to deliver to you a music matching party game fun for everyone.

Passport

Fit-Track

Looking for an app to help you stay in shape? Searching for a place to store a log of all of your favorite workout plans? Well look no further. Introducing, FitTrack. A full stack web application utilizing vanilla JS, Express, Node, and Mongoose to deliver a fully customizable experience for fitness enthusiasts everywhere.
